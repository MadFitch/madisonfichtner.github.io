Blizzard recently overhauled their News website, where a user may find any and all news regarding Blizzard. Battle.net hosts a specialized version of this website in the application, and it was one of my projects to coordinate the launch of the updated News page in the Battle.net News Tab.

Working alongside the Blizzard Web and Mobile team I coordinated the release of this experience in the new Battle.net BlizzCon Tab.

Engineering Design Project (RC Vehicle)

Through the course, Multidisciplinary Engineering Design, myself along with 3 other engineers designed, built, and used our Adafruit powered RC vehicle to complete an obstacle course.

The emphases of the project were to work as a team to experience the design process of a product and to successfully integrate all components of the design to meet a set of requirements. Our vehicle performed exceptionally well on the final course as all 4 of our components successfully came together. (Other disciplines on team were Mechanical Engineer, Computer Engineer, and Chemical Engineer)

As the computer scientist on the team, I was given the task of designing and implementing a user interface for controlling the vehicle. I decided to create a Android application that used Bluetooth Low Energy capabilities to send signals to our vehicles onboard microcontroller.
